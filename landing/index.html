How it works

From curiosity to deep understanding
in three steps

No courses to browse. No syllabus to design. Just describe what you want to truly understand — Enlightingale builds the path and walks it with you.

01

Create a Muse

Name a topic and set your current knowledge level. Enlightingale's Research Agent automatically finds the best sources and builds a tailored knowledge base.

02

Explore your Canvas

Your knowledge is synthesized into a visual Canvas — a structured overview with key concepts, timelines, comparisons, and insights drawn from across all your sources.

03

Learn with your Mentor

Your AI Mentor narrates the Canvas section by section, answers follow-up questions in real time, and adapts to wherever your curiosity takes you.

What's inside

Everything you need to go deep

Enlightingale combines autonomous research, structured knowledge synthesis, and real-time voice interaction into one focused learning space.

🔍

Research Agent

Automatically searches the web, evaluates sources, and curates a high-quality reading list — so you start learning immediately, not after hours of bookmarking.

🗂️

Knowledge Layer

Every source is read, summarized, and synthesized into a coherent knowledge layer: key concepts, a glossary, and cross-source insights — all in one place.

🖼️

Overview Canvas

Your Muse's knowledge is presented as a visual Canvas with typed sections — hero context, concept clusters, timelines, comparisons — ready for your Mentor to walk through.

🎙️

Voice Mentor

A warm, real-time voice tutor powered by Gemini 2.0 Flash Live. It narrates your Canvas, answers detour questions on the fly, and resumes exactly where you left off.
